Leatherworking Guide

Skilling · By Akor
If you're a fresh Rogue, keep every Rodent Pelt and Soft Hide you find. At character level 12 you can begin Leatherworking — craft everything into Flimsy Leather until level 5, then start making armour.

Thread is crafted at the Tailoring Station (6 Cotton each) — it sits right next to Leatherworking.

Levels 1–5 · Flimsy Leather

ItemMaterials
Flimsy Leather2 Rodent Pelts
Flimsy Coat15 Flimsy Leather · 1 Thread
Flimsy Striders12 Flimsy Leather · 1 Thread
Flimsy Boots10 Flimsy Leather · 1 Thread

Levels 5–15 · Plush Leather

ItemMaterials
Plush Leather2 Rodent Pelts · 1 Soft Hide
Plush Coat15 Plush Leather · 2 Thread
Plush Boots10 Plush Leather · 1 Thread

Levels 15–31 · Bone Dust

Bone Dust is the bottleneck. From level 15, hoard every Bone. Farming skeletons above the West Tunnel entrance is efficient.
  • Option A: Grind Fragile Leather to 20, then Delicate Leather to 30.
  • Option B: Grind Bone Dust until it stops giving XP (~level 31–35). Slower upfront but saves effort later.
Option B is recommended — one material, and you can farm Lumins at the same time.

Levels 35–45 · Graceful Leather

ItemMaterials
Graceful Leather2 Medium Hide · 1 Bone Dust
Graceful Coat15 Graceful Leather · 3 Heavy Leather Cord
Graceful Striders12 Graceful Leather · 2 Heavy Leather Cord
Graceful Boots10 Graceful Leather · 1 Light Leather Cord
